ABOUT BIMÍ

What We Do for Our Users

Bimí provides valuable, expert-authored, accurate and easy-to-understand health information in multimodal formats in indigenous African languages. We provide support for those who seek information and empower them with it. Our content is up-to-date and credible and our users can rely on that. Our vision is to to empower every African, everywhere – patient, caregiver or enthusiast, with information about disease, health and well-being and help them play active roles in all three.

Our Mission

To establish the most accessible, comprehensive and authoritative source of health information in Africa, for the lay person with little or no medical knowledge; providing high-quality, up-to-date health information relevant to local context, in easy-to-understand language and format such that education or lack of it wouldn’t prevent seekers from accessing it. Thus, bridging the patient-health care provider knowledge divide; strengthening the therapeutic alliance, correcting misinformation and making patients active stakeholders in their own care, health and well-being – a fundamental right of all patients.

Our Objectives

 Provide reliable, up-to-date, high-quality health information in lay terms, in a language our users speak at home.
 Provide health information relevant to the African context, information on diseases that are priority to us in Africa.
 Correct common myths, fads and rumors that may harm healthy practices/good health-seeking behavior.
 Encourage patients to take action by linking patients to good-quality health services.
 Foster sharing by the creation of patient/caregiver support groups through chat rooms/forums.

The Bimí Team

Our content at Bimí is carefully curated and simplified for a non-medical audience by our excellent editorial board. Our team combines the very best of medical knowledge, communication and language skills, digital artistry and content creation to bring you content unequalled in quality. Our company's efforts owe a great deal to our team of expert authors and volunteer writers who contribute immensely to content creation; while our translations leverage the unrivalled expertise of our friends at Novatia Translations, and best-in-class offline services built for us by our excellent friends at Alpha Technologies.
