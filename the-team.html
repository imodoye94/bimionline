Meet The Team

Godspower Oboli is a multiple award-winning medical doctor who graduated from the College of Medicine, University of Ibadan. A Young Innovation Leaders (YIL) Fellow, he possesses skills in innovation management and is committed to devising creative and strategic solutions to everyday problems. He is also an avid reader and a keen medical researcher with vast knowledge in genomics and bioinformatics. For relaxation, he enjoys singing and playing the piano. Dr. Oboli is a co-founder and the CEO/Editor-in-Chief at Bimí Online. 

Olasubomi (Subomi) is a medical doctor, voice over artist and skilled vocalist. He had his undergraduate medical education at the University of Ibadan, Nigeria, emerging in the top 2% of his class. He aspires to be a board certified Radiologist with skills in health informatics and imaging informatics. He is also an avid pet lover, with a strong liking for dogs. In his spare time, he enjoys making covers of popular songs and engaging in discussions about postgraduate medical education. Subomi is a co-founder, and the Chief Operations Officer at Bimí Online.

Dr. Onuoha Ihunayachukwu holds a Bachelor's degree in Medicine and Surgery from the University of Ibadan. He is a versatile health innovator, entrepreneur and youth leader who is very passionate about health system strengthening and  good governance. An accomplished public speaker and leader of thought, Dr. Onuoha brings his experience to bear on our board at Bimí Online where he is a co-founder and serves as the Chief Marketing Officer.

Dr. Aimiosior is the Director of Offline Media at Bimí Online. A young Healthcare Professional with both clinical and administrative experience, she obtained her MB;BS degree from the University of Ibadan, Nigeria, where she graduated top of her class. She is presently the Vice Lead - Health for The Neo-Child Initiative, a non-profit organization that promotes child health awareness and sustainable education to empower Nigerian Children. She also volunteers with Sustyvibes, an NGO redefining sustainability advocacy in Nigeria. She is passionate about strengthening Health Systems in Nigeria.

Dr. Oladejo is a Dental Surgeon who graduated from the College of Medicine, University of Ibadan, Nigeria. She is an oral health advocate who thoroughly enjoys the clinical practice of dentistry while maintaining a keen interest in public health especially the promotion of health and health related practices. In her spare time she enjoys hiking, watching movies and surfing the internet for fun facts. Dr. Oladejo is the Head of Content, Oral Health at Bimí Online.

Grace Agi, our Head of Content, Nutrition, is a brilliant public health nutritionist and an advocate for food and nutrition security among vulnerable groups. She is certified by the Institute of Dietetics in Nigeria as a Registered Dietitian-Nutritionist. Grace is experienced at managing public health nutrition projects in remote communities and Internally Displaced Persons Camps and has worked with organizations like the Doctors Health Initiative (DHI), Ramberg Child Survival Initiative (RACSI), Benue NGO Network and Impact Nutrition Africa Initiative to implement nutrition-related projects across Nigeria among vulnerable groups.

Olajumoke Oluwatoba is a Physiotherapist, with a keen interest in Orthopedic Physiotherapy. She is an award-winning writer and public speaker and uses these skills to promote health and spread health information. She also has interest in public health and primary health care especially in sub-Saharan Africa where the practice of Physical Therapy is gathering pace. She serves as the Head of Content, Physical Therapy at Bimí Online.

Imodoye Abioro is a Medical Doctor and fullstack computer programmer who graduated from the College of Medicine, University of Ibadan with a bachelor's degree in Medicine and Surgery. He is an award-winning serial social innovator and has co-founded four start-ups which have gone on to win many prestigious awards including a top-10 finish at the 2020 StartupXS Global Changemakers competition and Winning the Youth Assembly Global Impact Challenge 2020. He is a co-founder, Chief Technology Officer and Director of Innovation at Bimí Online.

Tunde Oyebamiji is a Maternal and Child health advocate and serial social enterpreneur. He is the co-founder and managing director of Future Food Project - a social venture tackling severe malnutrition in Africa. He has years of experience in maternal and child health advocacy and is a Harvard Crossroads Emerging Leader, Clinton Global Initiative University commitment maker and Resolution Fellow. He also co-founded Lend an Arm - Oyo State’s largest blood donation campaign. Tunde is committed to helping mothers and giving every child a fighting chance in Sub-Saharan Africa. He is the Director of Strategic Partnerships and Assistant Director of Innovation at Bimí Online.

Dr Kelvin Kanayo Ogili is a medical graduate of the College of Medicine, University of Ibadan who has a keen interest in entrepreneurship and public health financing. He is passionate about improving health literacy and serially organizes and volunteers in medical outreaches to remote, hard-to-reach communities providing health information through health talks in these communities and also screening these patients in the "last mile" for noncommunicable diseases. He looks forward to collaborating with relevant organisations in achieving Universal  health coverage in sub-Saharan Africa. He is the Director of Resources/Head of Recruitment at Bimí Online.

Oluwatobiloba Kazeem is a Medical Laboratory Scientist and excellent digital artist who has certifications in graphics design and image manipulation with expertise in both rasterization and vectorization technologies. He is an enthusiastic data scientist with certifications in Artificial Intelligence and Data Science from the Curators University and is a regular collaborator at the Coven Works digital laboratory. Oluwatobi is the Head of Content, Digital Media at Bimí Online and leads the team of digital artists who create 'Adventures of Nurse Bimí'.

Ifeanyi Muogbo is a Medical Doctor-in-training and fullstack developer with 6+ years experience in application layers, QA testing and frontend/backend development.  He is HarvardX certified in the CS50X Computer Science and Professional Mobile Applications Development programmes. He's also certified in the University of Helsinki's fullstackopen Reactjs program. He has in the past spearheaded successful transitions from LAMP to MERN/JAMStack and currently works mostly with the MERN Stack. He is quite versatile at REST/SOAP and GraphQL APIs, ReactNative for mobile development and has keen interests in serverless development, natural language processing, artificial intelligence, machine learning and genomic data science. He is our Head of Internet Resource Team at Bimí Online.
