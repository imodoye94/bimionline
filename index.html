WHAT IS BIMÍ


Bi mí (which means “Ask me” in Yoruba language) is a social venture. It is Africa’s first ever web-based, and mobile, health-related information aggregation platform. It is a website, mobile application, USSD (Unstructured Supplementary Service Data) and Toll-free phone service which hosts health information in multimodal formats (text, picture, audio and video). Information is available in multiple selected indigenous languages widely spoken across Africa; with all information authored for the consumption of the lay person assumed to have no prior medical knowledge and limited general literacy. Bi mí is an initiative aimed at improving the population health literacy and meet the specific health information demands of patients, caregivers and other health enthusiasts in Africa ABOUT US

WRITE FOR BIMÍ?

At Bimí we believe in harnessing the power and creativity of the youth to solve daunting problems, knowing that personal growth, development and self-actualization are the fundamental rewards of problem-solvers. Thus we are calling on all creative writers in the healthcare profession (healthcare professionals and healthcare professionals-in-training) to join our team of volunteer writers and create content for Bimí. All authors get full credit for their articles after it has completed the editorial process and is published. Writers can sign-up here
